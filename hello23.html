Mar Aboun monastery


The sites consist of one church and a hermitage located into natural cavities. A unique feature found in the mar aboun church is the presence of sacristy or “beit chimsho” in Syriac, and it is currently in restoration google maps


The convent of the cross

This is one of the most important cultural heritage sites of the valley dating back to the tenth century, it is the home to a one thousand year old painting of the crucified Jesus, three languages can be found in a rotten form Syriac, Strangello, Greek & Arabic. google maps


The golf of god
