Saint Antoine monastery


Kozhaya monastery of Saint Antoine the great. Kozhaya Syriac word meaning the living treasure. The historians and scholars support that this monastery was first build and began to be occupied by hermits at the beginning of the fours century. Its composed of church miraculous cave clattery museum and a guest house. The first printing press was in Kozhaya 1610. Currently two hermits live in this monastery, father Youhanna khawand and father Anthonios Rizk. google maps
