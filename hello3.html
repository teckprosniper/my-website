Kadisha valley is one of the most beautiful
places in Lebanon, I recommend you to visit it.
When you are in the valley, you feel peaceful,
happy and you forgot all your problems.
In this valley, you can practice many hobbies
like hiking, photographing, climbing cliffs,
discovering caves, going on a picnic and
much more. You can discover how
the ancients where living. Also you can try
local foods in some eco restaurant,
and you can spend a night in a guest house.
