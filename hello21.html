Patriarchate of Qannoubine


Qannoubine is the Syriac transcription of the Greek word kenobian meaning community. Qannoubine monastery served as patriarchate of the Maronite church from fifteenth to the nineteens century, the monastery church was built in the rock, and decorated with fresco from the eighteens nineteens century, the principal one represents the coronation of the Virgin Mary by the trinity with twelve Maronite patriarch looking on. At the entrance is a naturally preserved body of the patriarch Yousef tayan. google maps
